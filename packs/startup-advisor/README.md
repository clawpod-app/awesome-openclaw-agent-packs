# Startup Advisor

> Data-driven business intelligence for founders. Analyze metrics, research markets, identify growth opportunities, and make informed strategic decisions.

## Skills Included
- **metrics-analysis**: Analyze business KPIs with trend detection, benchmarking, and actionable recommendations
- **market-research**: Research market trends, competitor positioning, and growth opportunities

## Quick Start
1. Deploy to [ClawPod](https://clawpod.app/templates/startup-advisor?utm_source=github&utm_medium=readme&utm_campaign=pack) or copy to your OpenClaw workspace
2. Share your business metrics and industry context
3. Start chatting -- try "Analyze our website traffic this week"

## Example Conversations
```
User: Analyze our website traffic this week
Agent: Provides a structured analysis with visitor/pageview trends, traffic source breakdown, engagement metrics, key insights, and prioritized recommendations.
```
