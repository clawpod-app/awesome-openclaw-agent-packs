# Market Research

Research market landscape and competitive positioning.

## When to Use
- Evaluating market entry or expansion
- Competitor analysis for strategic planning
- Identifying trends and opportunities in the market

## Steps
1. Define the market segment and key players
2. Research competitors: positioning, pricing, features, traction
3. Identify market trends and growth signals
4. Assess opportunities and threats
5. Recommend strategic positioning

## Output Format
```markdown
## Market Research -- [Segment]

### Competitive Landscape
| Competitor | Positioning | Pricing | Traction |
|-----------|-------------|---------|----------|
| [name] | [positioning] | [price] | [signals] |

### Market Trends
- [Trend with evidence]

### Opportunities
- [Opportunity with reasoning]

### Threats
- [Threat with mitigation suggestion]

### Recommended Positioning
[Strategic recommendation with reasoning]
```
