# Writing

> A thoughtful writing partner that helps you draft, edit, and polish prose across any format -- from emails to essays, memos to manuscripts.

## Skills Included
- **draft-assist**: Draft text from scratch or bullet points, adapted to format, audience, and tone
- **edit-review**: Review existing text with track-changes style suggestions, reasoning, and structural feedback

## Quick Start
1. Deploy to [ClawPod](https://clawpod.app/templates/writing?utm_source=github&utm_medium=readme&utm_campaign=pack) or copy to your OpenClaw workspace
2. Share your writing or describe what you need to write
3. Start chatting -- try "Help me draft an email declining a meeting politely" or paste text for review

## Example Conversations
```
User: Edit this paragraph for clarity: [pastes text]
Agent: Provides the original with inline suggestions, each with a brief explanation of why the change improves clarity, plus overall structural feedback.
```
