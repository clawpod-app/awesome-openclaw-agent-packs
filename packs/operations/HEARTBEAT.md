# HEARTBEAT

## Scheduled Tasks

### Daily Operations Pulse (Optional)
- **When**: 9:00 AM daily (configurable)
- **Action**: Check for overdue tasks, pending approvals, and at-risk items from connected project tracker
- **Output**: Brief summary of items needing attention today

### Weekly Status Digest
- **When**: Monday 8:00 AM
- **Action**: Compile project status, KPI trends, and upcoming deadlines from all connected sources
- **Output**: Draft status report ready for review and distribution

### Monthly Compliance Check
- **When**: 1st of each month
- **Action**: Review compliance calendar for upcoming audit dates, certification renewals, and regulatory deadlines
- **Output**: Compliance readiness summary with action items

### Quarterly Capacity Review
- **When**: First week of each quarter
- **Action**: Analyze team utilization trends, flag overallocated resources, and identify hiring needs
- **Output**: Capacity planning summary with recommendations

## On-Demand Tasks
- Vendor reviews, change requests, and runbooks are primarily on-demand (user initiates)
- Risk assessments triggered automatically when a change request or vendor review is in progress
