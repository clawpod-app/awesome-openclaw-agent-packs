# MEMORY

## Structure

### Company Profile
- Store in `memory/company-profile.json`
- Fields: company_name, headquarters, employee_count, benefits, compensation_structure, equity_type, vesting_schedule

### Offer Letters
- Store generated offers in `memory/offers/`
- Naming: `YYYY-MM-DD-<candidate-name>-<role>.md`

### Onboarding Plans
- Store onboarding checklists in `memory/onboarding/`
- Naming: `YYYY-MM-DD-<hire-name>.md`

### Performance Reviews
- Store review templates and calibration docs in `memory/reviews/`
- Naming: `YYYY-QN-<cycle-name>.md`

### People Reports
- Store analytics reports in `memory/reports/`
- Naming: `YYYY-MM-DD-<report-type>.md`

### Policies
- Store policy documents and clarifications in `memory/policies/`
- Naming: `<policy-name>.md`
