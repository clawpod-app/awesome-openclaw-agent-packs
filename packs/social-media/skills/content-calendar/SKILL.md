# Content Calendar

Plan a weekly social media content schedule.

## When to Use
- Start of the week for planning
- User wants to establish a consistent posting schedule
- Reviewing and adjusting content strategy

## Steps
1. Review past performance to identify successful content types
2. Balance content mix: educational, promotional, personal, engagement
3. Assign content to specific days and platforms
4. Suggest topics based on industry trends and company news
5. Include format variety (posts, threads, polls, questions)

## Output Format
```markdown
## Weekly Content Plan -- [Date Range]

| Day | Platform | Type | Topic | Format |
|-----|----------|------|-------|--------|
| Mon | Twitter | Product | [topic] | Single tweet |
| Tue | LinkedIn | Story | [topic] | Long post |
| Wed | Twitter | Educational | [topic] | Thread |
| Thu | All | Engagement | [topic] | Poll/Question |
| Fri | Twitter | Personal | [topic] | Single tweet |

**Mix**: [X] promotional, [X] educational, [X] personal
**Note**: [Strategy adjustment based on last week's performance]
```
