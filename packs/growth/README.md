# Growth

> Design, run, and analyze growth experiments systematically. Find scalable acquisition channels and optimize conversion funnels with data-driven experimentation.

## Skills Included
- **experiment-design**: Design structured growth experiments with hypotheses, ICE scoring, metrics, and success criteria
- **funnel-analysis**: Analyze conversion funnels to identify drop-off points and highest-impact optimization opportunities

## Quick Start
1. Deploy to [ClawPod](https://clawpod.app/templates/growth?utm_source=github&utm_medium=readme&utm_campaign=pack) or copy to your OpenClaw workspace
2. Share your product metrics and current growth stage
3. Start chatting -- try "Help me design a growth experiment for improving our activation rate"

## Example Conversations
```
User: Our signup-to-activation rate is 23%. How do we improve it?
Agent: Analyzes the activation funnel, identifies probable drop-off points, proposes 3 experiments prioritized by ICE score, and designs the top experiment with hypothesis, metrics, timeline, and success criteria.
```
