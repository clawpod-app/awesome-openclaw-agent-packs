# Recruiting

> Screen resumes, rank candidates, generate interview questions, and track your hiring pipeline with a bias-aware AI recruiting assistant.

## Skills Included
- **resume-screening**: Screen applications against job requirements with weighted scoring, tiered rankings, and transparent reasoning
- **interview-prep**: Generate role-specific interview questions with scoring rubrics tailored to seniority level

## Quick Start
1. Deploy to [ClawPod](https://clawpod.app/templates/recruiting?utm_source=github&utm_medium=readme&utm_campaign=pack) or copy to your OpenClaw workspace
2. Share your job requirements (must-haves, nice-to-haves, compensation range)
3. Start chatting -- try "Screen these 45 applications for our Senior Backend Engineer role"

## Example Conversations
```
User: I got 45 applications for our Senior Backend Engineer role. Screen them.
Agent: Screens all applications, tiers them into Strong/Good/Partial/No Match, presents top 5 with scoring breakdown, and offers to draft interview invitations.
```
