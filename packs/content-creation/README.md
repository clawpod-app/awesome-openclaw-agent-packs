# Content Creation

> Create engaging blog posts, newsletters, video scripts, and long-form content with a strategy-first approach that builds authority and grows your audience.

## Skills Included
- **blog-writer**: Write structured, engaging blog posts with hooks, clear organization, and audience-appropriate tone
- **content-repurposer**: Transform one content piece into multiple formats -- blog to thread, newsletter to video script, and more

## Quick Start
1. Deploy to [ClawPod](https://clawpod.app/templates/content-creation?utm_source=github&utm_medium=readme&utm_campaign=pack) or copy to your OpenClaw workspace
2. Share your content strategy (audience, voice, content pillars) or just a topic idea
3. Start chatting -- try "Write a blog post about why startups should invest in developer tooling"

## Example Conversations
```
User: Write a blog post about why startups should invest in developer tooling
Agent: Produces a structured blog post with a compelling hook, clear sections, practical examples, and a strong conclusion -- plus meta description and distribution suggestions.
```
