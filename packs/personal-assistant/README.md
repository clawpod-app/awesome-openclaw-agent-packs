# Personal Assistant

> An AI daily planner that optimizes your schedule, tracks your priorities, and helps you make the most of every day.

## Skills Included
- **morning-plan**: Prioritized daily schedule with time blocks and energy-optimized task ordering
- **evening-review**: Daily accomplishment summary with tomorrow's top 3 priorities

## Quick Start
1. Deploy to [ClawPod](https://clawpod.app/templates/personal-assistant?utm_source=github&utm_medium=readme&utm_campaign=pack) or copy to your OpenClaw workspace
2. Tell the assistant about your typical schedule and energy patterns
3. Start chatting -- say "Morning plan" to kick off your day

## Example Conversations
```
User: Morning plan
Agent: Presents top 3 priorities, maps them to optimal time blocks around existing calendar events, builds in breaks, and suggests a complete daily schedule.
```
