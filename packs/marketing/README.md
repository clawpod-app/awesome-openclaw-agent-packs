# Marketing Soul Pack

> Create content, plan campaigns, and analyze performance across marketing channels. Maintain brand voice consistency, track competitors, and report on what's working.

## What It Does

- Draft blog posts, social media, email newsletters, landing pages, press releases, and case studies
- Generate full campaign briefs with objectives, channels, content calendars, and KPIs
- Review content against your brand voice, style guide, and messaging pillars
- Research competitors and generate positioning and messaging comparisons
- Build marketing performance reports with trends and optimization recommendations
- Run comprehensive SEO audits with keyword research and content gap analysis
- Design multi-email nurture sequences and drip campaigns

## Skills Included

| Skill | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `draft-content` | Draft blog posts, social media, email, landing pages, press releases, case studies |
| `campaign-plan` | Full campaign brief with objectives, channels, calendar, and success metrics |
| `brand-review` | Review content against brand voice and style guide |
| `competitive-brief` | Competitor research with positioning and messaging comparison |
| `performance-report` | Marketing performance report with metrics, trends, and recommendations |
| `seo-audit` | SEO audit -- keyword research, on-page analysis, content gaps, technical checks |
| `email-sequence` | Multi-email sequences for nurture flows, onboarding, and drip campaigns |
| `content-creation` | Content templates, writing best practices, SEO fundamentals, headline formulas |

## Example Conversation

```
User: I need a blog post about how AI is changing B2B marketing, targeting
      marketing directors at mid-market SaaS companies. Tone: authoritative
      but approachable. About 1200 words.

Agent: I'll draft that for you. Here's the structure:
  - Headline: "How AI Is Reshaping B2B Marketing (And What Smart Teams Do Differently)"
  - Hook: Open with a stat on AI adoption in marketing teams
  - Sections: Time savings, Personalization at scale, The human oversight imperative
  - CTA: Link to a related guide or demo
  [Generates full 1200-word draft with SEO-optimized subheadings]
```
