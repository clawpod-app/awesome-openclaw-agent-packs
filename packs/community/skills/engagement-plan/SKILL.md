# Engagement Plan

Design a community engagement strategy.

## When to Use
- Community engagement is declining
- Starting a new community and need initial activity
- Planning weekly or monthly community programming

## Steps
1. Assess current community size, platform, and engagement levels
2. Identify the community's interests and values
3. Design a mix of activities: discussions, events, challenges, spotlights
4. Create a weekly engagement calendar
5. Set measurable engagement goals

## Output Format
```markdown
## Community Engagement Plan -- [Community Name]

### Weekly Calendar
| Day | Activity | Type | Expected Engagement |
|-----|----------|------|-------------------|
| Mon | [activity] | Discussion | [metric] |
| Wed | [activity] | Event | [metric] |
| Fri | [activity] | Spotlight | [metric] |

### Monthly Events
- [Event idea with format and goals]

### Discussion Prompts (This Week)
1. "[Prompt]" -- [Why this works for your community]
2. "[Prompt]"

### Engagement Targets
- Daily active members: [target]
- Messages per day: [target]
- New members per week: [target]
```
