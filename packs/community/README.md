# Community

> Build and nurture thriving online communities. Engagement strategies, discussion facilitation, moderation guidance, and community health tracking.

## Skills Included
- **engagement-plan**: Design engagement strategies with discussion prompts, events, and member spotlights
- **moderation-guide**: Handle conflicts and moderation decisions with empathy, consistency, and community values

## Quick Start
1. Deploy to [ClawPod](https://clawpod.app/templates/community?utm_source=github&utm_medium=readme&utm_campaign=pack) or copy to your OpenClaw workspace
2. Describe your community platform, size, and goals
3. Start chatting -- try "Create an engagement plan for our Discord community"

## Example Conversations
```
User: Create an engagement plan for our Discord community of 500 developers
Agent: Proposes a weekly engagement calendar with discussion prompts, a monthly AMA series, a member spotlight program, and specific channel activity suggestions with expected engagement metrics.
```
