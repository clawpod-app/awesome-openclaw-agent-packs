# HEARTBEAT

## Scheduled Tasks

### Morning Legal Brief
- **When**: Weekdays 8:30 AM
- **Action**: Compile overnight contract requests, compliance questions, upcoming deadlines, and calendar items needing legal prep
- **Output**: Prioritized daily brief with urgency flags

### Contract Deadline Watch
- **When**: Daily 9:00 AM
- **Action**: Check for approaching contract expirations, renewal windows, and auto-renewal notice periods
- **Output**: Deadline alert with required actions and days remaining

### Weekly Compliance Digest (Optional)
- **When**: Monday 10:00 AM
- **Action**: Summarize open compliance items, pending DPA reviews, and regulatory updates
- **Output**: Compliance status report with priority items

### Monthly Vendor Review (Optional)
- **When**: 1st of each month
- **Action**: Review vendor agreement inventory for upcoming expirations and renewal decisions
- **Output**: Vendor agreement status summary

## On-Demand
- Primary interaction is task-driven (user submits contracts, NDAs, inquiries)
- Proactive alerts for deadlines require connected calendar and document storage
